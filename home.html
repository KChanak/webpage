Welcome to Our College

BV Raju Institute of Technology was established by Bhupatiraju Vissam Raju in 1997.
The institute was granted UGC-Autonomous status from the year 2014. BVRIT Narsapur is approved by the AICTE and the Government of Telangana.
The institute is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. BVRIT Narsapur is accredited by both NBA and NAAC, New Delhi. The institute is spread over a campus size of 110 acres. The faculty strength of the institute is 323 with 4704 students.
The institute offers a total of 21 courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. BVRIT Narsapur also offers a wide range of facilities and infrastructure to its students. The institute also offers scholarships and placement opportunities to its students.
BVRIT Narsapur has been placed in the rank band of 200-250 in the engineering category of NIRF ranking 2021. According to the official website of the institute, the vision of the institute is to create and nurture competent engineers and managers. The mission of the institute is to provide a positive and professional learning environment where students strive for excellence in becoming competent engineers.
