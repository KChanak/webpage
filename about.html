About BVRIT Narsapur

BV Raju Institute of Technology was established by Bhupatiraju Vissam Raju in 1997. The institute was granted UGC-Autonomous status from the year 2014. BVRIT Narsapur is approved by the AICTE and the Government of Telangana. The institute is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. BVRIT Narsapur is accredited by both NBA and NAAC, New Delhi. The institute is spread over a campus size of 110 acres. The faculty strength of the institute is 323 with 4704 students. The institute offers a total of 21 courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. BVRIT Narsapur also offers a wide range of facilities and infrastructure to its students. The institute also offers scholarships and placement opportunities to its students.

BVRIT Narsapur has been placed in the rank band of 200-250 in the engineering category of NIRF ranking 2021. According to the official website of the institute, the vision of the institute is to create and nurture competent engineers and managers. The mission of the institute is to provide a positive and professional learning environment where students strive for excellence in becoming competent engineers.

BVRIT Narsapur Admissions

The institute offers admissions at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. The courses in which BVRIT Narsapur admissions are offered are B.Tech, M.Tech and M.B.A. Admission to B.Tech programmes at BVRIT Narsapur is based on performance in the TS EAMCET and participation in the Telangana government's counselling process. BVRIT Narsapur admissions to M.Tech programmes are based on merit in the Post Graduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (TS PGECET) and participation in JNTU counselling. Admission to BVRIT Narsapur's M.B.A. programmes are based on performance in the TS Integrated Common Entrance Test.

BVRIT Narsapur Scholarships
Scholarships are given to students who have excelled in their academics. Needy and meritorious students can also apply for BVRIT Narsapur scholarships. The institute encourages its students to apply for states and national scholarships. Students at BVRIT Narsapur can apply for post-matric scholarships from the Telangana government.

BVRIT Narsapur Cut Off

The cut off for the B.Tech courses is prepared on the basis of marks obtained in TS EAMCET. Candidates whose names appear on the BVRIT Narsapur cut off list of the institution will be invited to appear for the counselling session. The closing home state rank for female-general category for B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning was 3562.1. The closing home state rank for male-general category for B.Tech Data Science was 4484.
